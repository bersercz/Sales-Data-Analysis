# Sales-Data-Analysis

 ## Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Sales Data Analysis using Power BI to uncover trends, key performance metrics, and business insights. The goal is to enable data-driven decision-making for stakeholders by analyzing sales performance across products, regions, customers, and time.


 **Objectives**

- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Analyze sales distribution across regions
- Track revenue growth over time
- Measure contribution of returning vs new customers
- Provide interactive visual insights for business decisions




**Dataset Information**

- The dataset includes:
- Product data
- Sales transactions
- Customer data
- Revenue & profit metrics
- Time dimension for trend analysis



**Tools & Technologies**

Pandas - Data Cleaning
SQL - Finding Insights
Power BI Desktop — Data modeling & visualization
DAX — Calculated fields & measures




**Key DAX Measures Used**

Total Revenue
Total Orders
Profit Margin %
YoY (Year over Year) Growth
Average Order Value
Product Contribution %



**Dashboard Features**

Region-wise sales map
Product category breakdown
Monthly and yearly revenue trend
Top & bottom performing products
Customer performance metrics
Interactive filters & slicers
